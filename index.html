The Journey

In the frozen lands of the Abyss, where the wind howled like the cries of lost souls and the snow blinded even the bravest warriors, a legend was born.

For centuries, the Frost Wyvern, a beast of unimaginable size and ferocity, ruled the icy peaks. Its scales shimmered like frozen diamonds, and its breath froze entire armies in their tracks. Many warriors had ventured to its lair atop Mount Cryoss, seeking glory, riches, or vengeance. None returned.
But Hex was different.

Hex was no ordinary fighter. Born into the harsh tundras, he grew up battling both the elements and the predators that roamed the frozen plains. With hair as white as snow and eyes that burned with a fierce amber light, he had earned his name after his unmatched ability to superior his enemies with cunning tactics and sheer willpower.

Word spread across the land of Hex’s intent to face the Frost Wyvern. Many mocked him, claiming he was no different from the hundreds who had perished before him. Yet, there was something about the way Hex carried himself calm, determined, and unshaken, that silenced doubters... 🗽
